How it works

1

Open Zephyr Scale

Navigate to your active Zephyr Scale test case in Jira and enter edit mode.

2

Paste Your Script

Click the Jira Helper extension icon, paste your Markdown test steps, and click "Parse Setup" to validate the layout.

3

Auto-Fill Steps

Set a custom delay (e.g., 1.0s) and hit "Fill Steps". Sit back while the extension types everything into the step-by-step editor for you.
